What is this?!!

What, Where and When is Bundoora City Hacks?

The hackathon will be held over a weekend, and it will bring together students from various schools in Bundoora. The participants will be challenged to come up with innovative solutions to problems faced by the local community.

The event will be focused on using technology to develop practical and creative solutions to real-world problems.The Bundoora City Hacks event will be organized to encourage young people to use their skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in their community.

The participants will be encouraged to think outside the box and come up with creative solutions to problems that may have previously seemed unsolvable.During the event, the students will be mentored by mentors from various fields, including technology, engineering, and entrepreneurship. The mentors will provide guidance and support to the participants throughout the event, helping them to refine their ideas and turn them into viable solutions.

The hackathon will(hopefully) see a variety of projects being developed, ranging from mobile apps and websites to physical devices and gadgets. The participants will present their projects to a panel of judges, who will evaluate them based on their creativity, feasibility, and potential impact on the community.

The Bundoora City Hacks event is expected to be a huge success, with participants and mentors alike praising the event's organization and execution. The event will provide an excellent opportunity for high school students to showcase their skills, collaborate with peers, and gain valuable experience in problem-solving and innovation.In conclusion, the Bundoora City Hacks event will be an excellent initiative that will provide a platform for high school students to develop their skills and showcase their talents.

The event's focus on solving real-world problems and using technology to do so will be both timely and impactful, and it is hoped that more similar events will be organized in the future to encourage and inspire the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers.
